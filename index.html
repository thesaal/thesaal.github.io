Meet Saal

“I’ve heard a lot about Kashmiri wazwan and the Goshtaba, but I don’t know where to find it!” is what most people we meet tell us. With limited options to find authentic Kashmiri food, most people never even taste the wonder of the succulent meat and gravies that form the backbone of the cuisine.

Saal is run by a team of passionate foodies from kashmir who want to share their love for the legendary cuisine by making it accessible to everyone conveniently. Our menu has been specially curated to serve you with the best of Kashmiri cuisine. All dishes come with a complimentary serving of rice and special condiments for a finger-licking and authentic experience which you can eat anywhere, anytime.
